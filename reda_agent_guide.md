# Reda Agent — Your day in the app

This is a one-pager. Read it once, keep it. Most days, you'll do steps 2–4.

---

## 1. Sign in (first time only)

- You'll receive your email and a temporary password from Uzo on WhatsApp DM.
  Don't share these.
- Open the Reda app → enter the email + temp password → **Sign in**.
- The first thing you do: tap your **Profile** tab (bottom right) → if there's
  a "change password" option, set a real password. (If not yet, ask Uzo.)
- Grant the notification permission when the app asks. Without it, you
  won't get pinged when a new delivery is assigned to you.

You don't need to sign out at the end of the day — the app remembers you.

---

## 2. Today's deliveries

Open the app → **Today** tab (home icon).

At the top, two numbers:

- **Earned today** — total ₦ Reda owes you for what you've already delivered.
- **Deliveries** — how many you've completed out of today's total.

Each individual stop card shows the price for that single trip — that's the number you ask the customer for at the door.

Below that, your stops list. The order is: **what just moved is on top**.
Any delivery whose status changed most recently (you just flagged it, you
just marked it not answering, ops just replied to your flag) bubbles up.
Completed / closed deliveries fall to the bottom.

Tap any row to open the delivery.

---

## 3. Doing a delivery

Tap a delivery in *Today* → you see the full details:

### Before you set out
- Tap **Call [customer first name]** to phone them.
- Tap **Open in maps** to navigate to the address.
- Read the **Vendor** notes if there are any (warning-yellow box) — that's
  Uzo flagging something special about this client.

### When the customer doesn't answer
Tap **Update status** at the bottom. Pick one:

- *Not answering* — they didn't pick up at all.
- *Number busy* — line is busy, try again later.
- *Switched off* — phone is off.
- *Tomorrow* — they want it tomorrow.
- *Postponed* — they want it later this week.
- *Follow up* — needs a real follow-up call later.

These are *soft fails*. The delivery stays in your list; you can call back.
Provide a short reason if asked.

### When you need Uzo or a dispatcher to step in

Use the **alert** icon in the top-right of the delivery (the small red
triangle next to the **?**) — that's your direct line to ops for things
like:

- *Wrong address* — customer says the address you have is wrong.
- *Can't reach client* — phone is dead and you've already tried Not answering.
- *Payment dispute* — customer is arguing about the price.
- *Product issue* — wrong product, damaged, etc.
- *Other* — anything else.

1. Tap **alert** → pick the chip → optional short note ("address is in
   Lekki not VI, customer said over the phone").
2. **Send to ops**.

One tap does two things: opens a chat thread on this delivery AND moves
the status (so you don't also need to hit Update status). Uzo gets a push.
When he replies, you get a push back — open the delivery, the reply is
right under the **Messages** section.

The thread stays alive until the delivery is delivered / cancelled / etc.
Don't use the alert button for things you can fix yourself with Update
status — use it when you actually need ops to call the customer or sort
something out.

### When you've delivered

Tap **Mark delivered** (the red button at the bottom).

1. Confirm quantity delivered. Defaults to what was ordered.
2. Enter how much the customer actually paid (defaults to the expected total).
3. Pick **Cash** or **Transfer**.
4. Tap **Confirm delivery**.

If the customer paid more or less than expected, the app warns you and
records the actual amount. Reda will reconcile.

### Wrap up before 11:59pm

At **11:59pm Lagos** the app automatically rolls anything still pending to the
next working day (Sunday is skipped — Saturday's pending stops land on
Monday). So mark your last deliveries before 11:59pm if you finished them
today. Anything you mark after 11:59pm might be on a row that just got
rolled — you'll see a "delivery has been deleted" error and need to call
Uzo to sort it out.

### Stand-by / Delivery-closed pushes

Sometimes Uzo assigns the same delivery to a few agents so whoever gets
there first delivers it. Two pushes you might see:

- **"Stand by — Funke is on Emmanuel. Hold for now."**
  Another agent just marked en-route. Don't call the customer yet;
  wait. Your row stays open in case Funke can't make it.
- **"Delivery closed — Funke delivered to Emmanuel. Your row is closed."**
  Another agent already delivered. You don't need to do anything — the
  row is now cancelled in your list.

### "Not my route" — when a row shouldn't be on your list

Sometimes a delivery shows up on your list that isn't yours — the wrong agent
was named in the forward, or it's outside your area. Tap the **⚠ flag** on the
delivery and choose **"Not my route"**.

- This **doesn't change the order's status or close it** — it just alerts the
  office. Uzo or a dispatcher reassigns the order to the right agent, so it
  stays alive and still gets delivered.
- You can add a short note ("not my customer", "wrong area") — this helps Uzo
  spot patterns in the contractor's forwards.
- Once ops reassigns it, the row simply drops off your list — nothing else for
  you to do.

Don't use **Customer Cancelled** for this — that one means "the customer killed
the order" and it closes everyone else's row too.

Both mean: stand down. Treat them as good news (one less stop).

### Tap fires offline too
If you don't have signal, mark the delivery as usual — the app queues it
and uploads as soon as you're back online. A yellow strip at the bottom
of the screen shows *"Offline · N queued"*. Don't worry about it.

## 4. My Stock

Tab → **My stock** (package icon).

What you have on hand, per product. Red = negative (you owe stock back),
yellow = ≤3 units left.

If your physical count doesn't match, **tell Uzo by WhatsApp** with the
product name + the difference. Don't try to "fix" by marking deliveries
you didn't actually do.

---

## 5. Earnings

Tab → **Earnings** (wallet icon).

The big number at the top is **this week's** earnings. Reda pays on
Fridays.

- **Today** = what you've earned today.
- **This month** = month-to-date.

Below that, the list of every delivery you've completed this week with the
exact ₦ amount for each.

If you think a delivery should be there and isn't, you probably forgot
to **Mark delivered**. Open it from *Today* and complete the action.

## 6. Call your admin / dispatch / a teammate

Reda has phone calls built in. You don't need to use your own SIM or save anyone's number. Audio is encrypted; nothing is recorded.

**To make a call:**
1. Open **Profile** (user icon at the bottom).
2. Tap **Team directory**.
3. Tap the green phone next to the person you want.

**To receive a call:**
- Your phone rings with your normal ringtone, even when the screen is locked.
- Tap **Accept** to talk or **Decline** to send them to voicemail (well, "missed").
- A Bluetooth headset works for accept/end too.

**First time you use it:**
- Android asks "Allow Reda to make and manage calls?" — say **Allow**. Otherwise calls won't ring properly.
- It also asks for the microphone — Allow that too.
- If you tapped no by mistake, open Android **Settings → Apps → Reda → Permissions** and turn them on.

**Past calls:**
- Profile → **Call history** shows your last 50 calls. Red = missed / declined / cancelled. Tap the green phone to call them back.

**Battery saver killing your ringer?**
Some phones (Tecno, Infinix, Xiaomi, Realme) shut down apps in the background to save battery. If your phone isn't ringing for incoming calls:
- Android Settings → Apps → Reda → **Battery → Unrestricted**
- Also enable **Autostart** and **Display over other apps** if your phone has those toggles.

## 7. Profile + sign out

Tab → **Profile** (user icon).

If you ever need to switch accounts or hand the phone to someone:

- Open Profile → **Sign out** at the bottom.
- If there are unsynced changes, the app will warn you. Don't sign out
  until you're back online and the yellow strip is gone.

---

## When the app is being weird

1. Pull down on the list to refresh.
2. Close the app fully (swipe up from the bottom, swipe Reda away). Reopen.
3. If still weird, **WhatsApp Uzo with a screenshot** describing what you
   tried. Don't make up data to "fix" it.
